What is Chinese New Years?

Chinese New Years is a holiday celebrated annually sometime between January 21 and February 20th. The date is based on the lunar calendar; the date falls on the second new moon after the winter solstice. The purpose of Chinese New Years is to remove the bad luck and welcoming the new luck.

What is a Red Packet?

A red packet (红包) is a gift given to friends and family. Usually red packets are filled with money to symbolize good wishes and good luck ahead. However, the design on the red packet symbolizes good luck and prosperity.
