History


There were many dynasites of China, but the earliest evidence of China's civilization dates back to 2205 BCE. This would be known as the Xia dynasty.
- Xia Dynasty (2205-1575)
- Shang Dynasty (1570-1045)
- Zhou Dynasty (1045-265)

Historical Landmarks

The Great Wall of China A famous landmark and a must to visit. The Great Wall of China was built in 220 B.C for the purpose of defending themselves from the north.
The Forbidden City Created in 1406, its name was given because most subjects of the realm were prohibited from going into this city.
The Summer Palace The original Summer Palace was created in 1750. Though destroyed by a war, it was restored in 1886.
