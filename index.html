CHINA

Where?


China is located in East Asia. China is bordered by Russia to the north, Japan to the east, Korea to the south, and the Philippines to the west. China also borders Mongolia. The capital of China is Beijing. Stretching 3,100 miles (5,000 kilometers) from east to west and 3,400 miles (5,500 kilometers) from north to south, China is a large country with widely varying landscapes.

Population


China is special because it is one of the most populated countries in the world (2nd most). China has a rough population of 1.4 billion since 2021. China owns over 3,700,000 square miles of land.
